Personal Projects

Since leaving sixth form, I have worked during breaks from study to teach myself Python, and apply it to at home projects to develop my skills

In between my second and third year of university I made an app hosted on streamlit that uses APIs to calculate the estimated time for a solar panel to payback the initial installation and purchase costs from the incurred energy savings.

It does this using details specific to the user, including their location, energy costs and energy usage.

Algorithmic Forex Trading Bot | Summer 2024

Between my first and second year I made an algorithmic trading bot, which employs multiple different forex trading strategies via an oanda demo trading account. It also shows the backtested performance of each strategy.

Personal Website & Local Weather Tracker | Gap Year 2022-2023

During my gap year, I followed Angela Yu's 100 Days of Coding python course to teach myself to code in python. This allowed me to build an early project that simply scraped weather data from nearby weather stations to retrieve information about my local area
