Tutoring

I also work part-time as a Physics and Maths Tutor for a range of years and exam boards.

I offer personalised lesson plans and interactive tutoring sessions to Physics students Year 13 and below, as well as Mathematics tutoring for Year 11 and below, find me on MyTutor

Who do I tutor?

I am happy to tutor any maths or physics student, I even have some experience tutoring maths to mature students.

For Physics, I tutor any A-Level or below students for any exam board, although I have experience teaching Edexcel, AQA and OCR.

For Maths, I mainly tutor GCSE and below, however I am open to teaching A Level Maths students. Also for any exam board

My Qualifications

  • DBS Checked as of Oct 2024
  • Achieved a first in the second year of my Warwick Physics degree
  • Experience teaching AQA, Edexcel and OCR
  • Achieved A*AA at A-Levels in Mathematics, Physics and History
  • Began tutoring in Year 12 as part of my Duke of Edinburgh volunteering programme

Pricing

I charge competitive prices for every session following a first free to introduce myself and make sure we are a good fit. I charge:

  • £25 per hour for in person (Northamptonshire only).
  • £20 per hour for online tutoring*.

* - For online tutoring I use Zoom, this allows me to use interactive whiteboards that I can share with students and offers easy meeting management.

Currently a tutee on MyTutor?

If you currently are a student on MyTutor, please consider contacting me to organise private lessons for yourself or a student instead, why?:

  • Boosting interactivity, privately, I tutor through Zoom, enabling myself and students to have constant access to an interactive whiteboard, instead of being constrained to only using it during sessions, as on MyTutor.
  • Communication made easier, once a relationship of trust has been built, the safeguarding focus of the chat function on MyTutor goes from diligent to restrictive. By considering private tutoring, communication with both the student and parent/guardian at the same time becomes much easier.
  • Get the same for less, MyTutor takes about half of what you pay for lessons, for online private lessons I offer £20ph, with no middleman paying for tutoring becomes easier to afford.
